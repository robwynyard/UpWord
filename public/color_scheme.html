Earth Tone Usage Guidelines

Charcoal Black (#0A0908)

Primary text color for maximum readability. Use for headings, body text, and high-contrast elements that need attention.

Slate Blue (#22333B)

Primary brand color for headers, main buttons, and important UI elements. Creates trust and sophistication.

Warm Cream (#EAE0D5)

Page backgrounds and subtle section backgrounds. Creates a warm, inviting atmosphere without being distracting.

Warm Beige (#C6AC8F)

Accent elements, borders, and call-to-action buttons. Adds warmth and draws attention to important actions.

Dark Brown (#5E503F)

Hover states, emphasis elements, and secondary accents. Provides depth and richness to the design.

Pure White (#FFFFFF)

Card backgrounds, form fields, and clean content areas. Provides contrast and breathing space in the design.
